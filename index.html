Our Story

For 18 years, Bella Vista Trattoria has been run by one family in the heart of Hayes Valley. What began as a small neighborhood dining room is now a San Francisco institution for people who love real Italian food without the fuss.

We've never chased trends. We just keep rolling pasta by hand, firing the oven, and pouring good wine for the neighbors who've become friends.
